<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="Why can't I create a goal?" icon="fire">
        Only managers and admins can create goals. This is to ensure that goals are aligned with the company's objectives and that everyone is held accountable for their goals.
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Why don't I see X integration?" icon="plug">
        If you don't see an integration, it's either because it's not available or it's not enabled for your company.
        If an integration is disabled, reach out to your admin to enable it. Otherwise reach out to [support@vork.app](mailto:support@vork.app) to request a new integration..
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title="Why wasn't my event logged?" icon="plug">
        Ensure that you have properly tagged your activities with the correct tags and also verify that you have integrated with the correct services.
        If you're still having issues, reach out to [support@vork.app](mailto:support@vork.app) for help.
    </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
