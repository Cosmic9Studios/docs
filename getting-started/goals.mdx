## How it works

<Steps>
    <Step title="Set a goal">
        In the input field, enter the goal you want to achieve.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Select an Approver">
        Select a manager who will acknowledge once you've achieved your goal.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Create the Goal">
        Click the "Create Goal" button to create your goal.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Achieve your Goal">
        Once you've achieved your goal, let your manager know so they can acknowledge it on the Vork platform. Once acknowledged, you will receive stars issued by your manager.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## Acknowledging Goals


<Frame caption="Managers view when they're assigned as an approver">
    <img src="/images/goal-achieved.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
</Frame>

If you are a manager, you can acknowledge your team members' goals by clicking on the "Goal Achieved" button. You will be prompted to enter the number of stars you want to issue to the team member. Once you've issued the stars, the team member will receive a notification.
