## What are Objectives?

Objectives are the high-level goals that you want the company. They are the big picture that you are working towards. Objectives are typically set for a longer period of time, such as a quarter or a year.

## How they work in Vork

Vork has a 3 tier system for goals:

1. Objectives
2. Group Goals
3. Personal Goals.

Objectives are the highest level of goals in Vork. Group Goals are goals that are set for a team or department. Personal Goals are goals that are set for an individual.

Personal Goals are tied to Group Goals, which are tied to Objectives. This means that when you achieve a Personal Goal, it contributes to the Group Goal, which in turn contributes to the Objective.

## Why they are important

Objectives are important because they give you a clear direction of where you are going. They help you to focus on what is important and to prioritize your work. Objectives also help to align the team and the company towards a common goal.

## How to set Objectives

To set Objectives in Vork, you need to have the *Admin* or *Leader* role.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to objective creation">
        Navigate to the Objectives page on the Vork Dashboard and click on the *Create Objective* button.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Fill in the details">
        Fill in the details for the Objective, such as the title, description, and due date.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Create the Objective">
        Click on the *Create* button to create the Objective.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## How to edit Objectives

To edit Objectives in Vork, you need to have the *Admin* or *Leader* role.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Navigate to the objective page">
        Navigate to the Objectives page on the Vork Dashboard and click on the Objective that you want to edit.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Go to the edit page">
        On teh Objective page, click *Edit* in the dropdown menu with the three dots.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Edit the details">
        Edit the details of the Objective, such as the title, description, and due date.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Save the changes">
        Click on the *Update* button to save the changes to the Objective.
    </Step>
</Steps>

## How to track Objectives

Anyone in Vork can track Objectives, but only the *Admin* or *Leader* role can update the status of the Objective.

To track an Objective navigate to the Objectives page. There you will see a list of objectives as well as the overall progress of the Objective.
