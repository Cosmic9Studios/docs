Billing it managed by Stripe. If you would like to switch the frequency of billing (ie. Monthly to Yearly) you can do so in the portal from the Admin > Billing page.

### Prorated Billing

As users are added or removed, billing is prorated based on active users. If you have any questions regarding billing. Reach out to: billing@vork.app

<Note>Active users are users who have activated their account by accepting the invite. You will not be charged for pending users.</Note>
